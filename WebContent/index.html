ABOUT INSTITUTE

Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT), Gandhinagar, named after the founder of Reliance Group late Dhirubhai Ambani, was established in 2001. With focus on higher education and private participation, it successfully represents the Wave-4 of educational advancement in Gujarat.



VISION, MISSION & OBJECTIVES



VISION

All students will be physically educated, have motor skills to perform a variety of physical activities and pursue a healthy and active lifestyle in an increasingly changing world. In DAIICT sports and games are an important and unique part of the daily programme. Not only does it contribute to the overall goals of education, but also contributes to the development and maintenance of fitness, motor skills, social skills and health. The sports department of our institute has always endeavoured to fulfil the basic objectives of sports and games by providing the appropriate infrastructural facilities and the right kind of motivation to aspiring students.




MISSION

  • Develop physical talents to their maximum potential.
  • Engage in competitive activities, while promoting sound health, safety, and physical fitness.
  • Exemplify good conduct as a means for learning good citizenship.
  • Learn to appropriately experience both success and failure in an educational environment.
  • Learn from experience that consequences follow the violation of a rule.
  • Experience working as a team member, exercising self-discipline and self-sacrifice in order to achieve team goals.


OBJECTIVES

  • Physical fitness and skills
  • Mental alertness
  • Ethical qualities
  • Constructive social abilities
  • Emotional maturity
